Ear Care



You should control and apply ear care to your dog every week. Ears should be cleaned with a wet cotton and after it should be dried with a towel slowly. When you are bathing your dog (once a month) you should plug a cotton with Vaseline inside the ear of your dog to not get water inside.

If your dog has long ears it should be aired every ten minutes in order to avoid ear infection. Also, ears should be cleaned with ear lotions, not with cotton bud because it could harm the eardrum. Moreover, the biggest mistake in ear care is to leave the ear moisture after cleaning it because it should be dried properly.
