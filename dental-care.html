Dental Care



​Dental care should be applied to your dog at least 2-3 times a week. You can do it by yourself or you can go to a veterinary and they can do it there.

If you are doing by yourself, you can buy toothpastes for dogs and children toothbrush with soft ends. Also, if your dog is not used to brushing their teeth don’t brush all of their teeth in once, do it slowly and make sure you dog is getting accustomed.
