Hi, I'm Nurbanu Büyüktuna

I am a married woman with one son and four pets. I dreamed of doing a job related with animals since my childhood; however, when I graduated from Istanbul University, my life shaped in the direction of an academic career. I started working at the human resources department of certain firms. Even when I was working in these firms, I thought about my dream of doing a job with dogs. In my childhood I was really interested with animals, I used to save cats from streets and help dogs etc. Thus, one day I realized that working at a firm is not what I want and working at human resources department kind of helped me figure out what I want. Therefore, I went to Russia to get an education towards my interests and after, I opened a pet stylist saloon in Etiler. In this small business, I would name myself as a dog beauty specialist. With my team, we apply several practices to dogs such as shaving, coloring, spa treatment, and skin care.

Working with dogs has always been my enthusiasm since my childhood. I feel like they are my child and I take care of them like a mother take cares of her children. I believe that I have a special bond with them.
